hi there! welcome to the masterlist for my series "media wrapped."

in each entry of this series, i will be listing out the media i experienced during a specific timeframe, and my general impressions/feelings of it (positive or negative, or something else entirely). consider it a summary of what i was keeping up with at the time!

**⚠ IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER: please do not confuse this series for a list of recommended media.** if you are expecting glowing reviews and praise of everything, i strongly suggest that you readjust your expectations (or to check out [[current brain rotation | this page of my favorite media]]). my lived experiences influence how i read certain media, and part of that means that i will not find everything to be my cup of tea. and thats OK!

personally, i find it fun to write think pieces on media. i love asking myself questions like:

- "did the author tell the story they wanted to tell effectively?" or
- "how did they handle the subject matter? how did it make me feel?" or
- "what moments resonated with me the most?" / "what moments felt lacking despite their supposed build-up"
- etc.

i know full well that people are not going to 100% agree with what i have to say, and that's fine! whenever i come across these types of discussions online, i sometimes find it valuable to see what others have to say (and anything i may have overlooked on my first viewing of it). so i hope that if you are reading these, you extend the same respect to me.

anyways, go ham reading these. i hope that you can discover something new, and that it helps broaden your perspective!
