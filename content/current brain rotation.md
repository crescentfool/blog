## games
### persona 3
THIS HAS BEEN MY LONG TIME FAVORITE GAME SINCE IT BECAME A PART OF MY LIFE IN AUGUST 2021!!! I LOVE PERSONA 3!!! i think everyone knows this about me but it bears repeating nevertheless!

the way persona 3 handles the themes of life and death has made it a cornerstone in my life... whenever i go through hardship, i find myself thinking about this game to give me hope. anyone who looks at my socials for five minutes will know that my favorite characters are the persona 3 protagonist (minato arisato the love of my life) and ryoji mochizuki. they're both such good representations of the themes of the game (in conjunction with aigis, they would not be the characters they are without her).

but favoritism aside, i think that all of the cast members are exceptional (YUKARI TAKEBA THE GIRL THAT YOU ARE!!! she's so realistic with how she handles her grief, it's refreshing). there's a lot to love about this game and it's setting and i find something to love about the characters every time i go through it.

for those who are curious: my favorite version of persona 3 is FES. while i have experience with reload (100% SL + all trophies except the 50k yen one) and portable (watched a playthrough of it), the graphics of FES + the janky mechanics hold a special place in my heart...

### splatoon (series)
i've loved this series since it's debut on the wii u in 2015! while i was a very casual player back then, i was entranced by the music and funny little character designs. it wasn't until splatoon 3 that i got *hooked* on this series. i think i have at least 1.7k hours across all the splatoon games 🤯

i love this series for a multitude of reasons, including, but not limited to:
- the gameplay: ITS SOOO FUN and there's so much to learn! i am especially fond of salmon run, but i find the gameplay loop to be enjoyable in both PVP and solo content.
- the worldbuilding: the stages in this game do an exceptional job at getting you immersed in the world and imagining the everyday lives of the characters. and if you go into the artbooks... there will be more to love...!!!
- the fashion: i owe my entire life to toni kensa. but seriously though, there's such a nice assortment of gear available, it's like i'm playing dress ups...
- the music: SUCH A BOP!!!! my personal favorite bands are h2whoa, chirpy chips, and sashimori.

i like to play a lot of diferent weapons, but my top favorites are: squiffer, octobrush, and inkbrush!

### sonic the hedgehog (series)
sonic has been an on-and-off interest since... 2009? 2010? i only really started getting more invested in it in late 2024 with the release of sonic x shadow generations (and watching sonic boom a few months beforehand).

there is something so inspiring to me about how this series has continued to persist for the past three decades despite some of the games not being "hits." i personally think it's because sonic's core characteristics have stayed the same regardless of what game you're playing. he embodies the "cool" factor very well, but i also feel that sonic really encourages you to live your most authentic life in the present?! and that's just really cool to me...

**my personal favorite entries:** sonic colors (ds and wii), sonic and the black knight, sonic 06, sonic generations
