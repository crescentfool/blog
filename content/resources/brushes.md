a list of some of my favorite brushes, and some resources for finding new assets!

**⚠ IMPORTANT NOTE:** my software of choice is clip studio paint! while some brushes i am sharing are available to use in multiple programs (photoshop brushes are `.abr` and can be read by both clip studio paint and procreate), some of these are exclusive to CSP (usually on the asset store, OR noted as `.sut`).

## all-time favorites
these are brushes that i use in most of my artworks, i consider them to be a pivotal part of creating my signature look!

### momo storybook set (CSP)
DOWNLOAD LINK: https://assets.clip-studio.com/en-us/detail?id=1761190

I LOVE the texture of these brushes, they're great for a very traditional look! i originally learned of them through [noctilin](https://noctilin.straw.page/), who recommended the brush [in this post](https://noctilin.tumblr.com/post/705973838657323008/hey-if-you-dont-mind-me-asking-what-brushes-do).

i like to use **momo chalky** to lay down flats, and **momo doodler** and **momo sprinkle** to add additional texture. momo and cream is my least used brush of the four, but i still find uses for it!

**examples of the brush in my own personal work:** [1](https://crescentfool.tumblr.com/post/777125130588585984/its-that-time-of-year-again), [2](https://crescentfool.tumblr.com/post/738437790412521472/december-31st-but-i-make-it-silly-based-on-a), [3](https://crescentfool.tumblr.com/post/719635178511892480/super-excited-for-the-p3-remake-cant-wait-to)

### pufuu's splatoon brush pack (procreate, CSP)
this one is funny to me because the original brush set was made for procreate by [pufuu](https://www.youtube.com/@Pufuu), as part of [a video tutorial on how to create drawings with the splatoon art style](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2osfZyRnLRE).

here's a few places where you can download the brushes:
- [procreate brush set](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1Lns8vWhY-ISrwYw1MltNKI6k7fckNAMc/view): the original brush set as made by pufuu, for procreate!
- [clip studio paint version by hychlorions](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1GeVIC75mLeCab2KBxmiVVelFRXhafno5): a port of the brushes by hychlorions, you can reshare [their original post here](https://hychlorions.tumblr.com/post/699924712256634881/if-anyone-else-has-watched-this-tutorial-for)! this is the one that i use.
- [clip studio paint version by splatdualies](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1oMauUwLhppCn5EiksIxfeWSfXn-gZ3UQ/view): another port of pufuu's brush to CSP, this time [done by splatdualies on bsky](https://bsky.app/profile/splatdualies.bsky.social/post/3lke2a2c2gk2p)! i thought this one was worth noting because it also includes script brushes, which uses ardnin's splatoon fonts.

of this brush set, my favorites are:
- **splat brush line pen** - great for linework! this is where the crunchy edges of my art tend to come from.
- **splat brush regular** - great for blocking in big shapes.
- **splat texture DX** - great for applying gradients and fades of color! it has a nice look to it.

i tend to use the splat brush line pen in conjunction with [the ice cream tool](https://assets.clip-studio.com/en-us/detail?id=1759448) (a great tool for filling in flats).

if you don't want to download the brushes but still like the look of the splat brush line pen, you can probably get a comparable look by [changing your brush to these settings listed by eela](https://eela.tumblr.com/post/757307440367894528/lovvveee-your-art-the-colors-r-so-gorgeous-and)!

**examples of the brush in my own personal work:** [1](https://bsky.app/profile/crescentfool.bsky.social/post/3lix7jkna6k2u), [2](https://bsky.app/profile/crescentfool.bsky.social/post/3ldroabwiuc2b), [3](https://bsky.app/profile/crescentfool.bsky.social/post/3lcvsakr2uc2v)

### THE ICE CREAM TOOL (CSP)
**DOWNLOAD LINK:** https://assets.clip-studio.com/en-us/detail?id=1759448

ARE YOU: SICK OF FILLING IN FLATS MANUALLY, AND HAVE DECENTLY CLEAN LINE ART? THIS IS FOR YOU!!!

literal time saver. cannot recommend this enough.

### Graininess Highlights (CSP)
**DOWNLOAD LINK:** https://assets.clip-studio.com/en-us/detail?id=1859789

costs 10 CLIPPY, keep an eye out for this whenever there's CSP log-in bonuses! this is basically an airbrush that has noise particles/grain at the edges. i like using this both as a soft light source, and an eraser to soften up shadows. similarly to the momo storybook set, i discovered this brush through [noctilin's brush recommendation post](https://noctilin.tumblr.com/post/705973838657323008/hey-if-you-dont-mind-me-asking-what-brushes-do).

**examples of the brush in my own personal work:** [1](https://crescentfool.tumblr.com/post/771359807394512896/my-longest-friend-and-companion), [2](https://crescentfool.tumblr.com/post/757639941773803522/stages-of), [3](https://crescentfool.tumblr.com/post/757732857551716352/ezekiel-moon-for-darknicjen)

## other places to find brushes
while i may not have used all of these in my own works, these have been recommended by other people, and i thought it would be worthwhile to list!

### general brush sources
- [CSP asset store](https://assets.clip-studio.com/en-us/) 
- [Devin Elle Kurtz](https://tamberella.gumroad.com/?sort=most_reviewed): devin is a wonderful visdev and background artist, the brushes she makes is specifically made with photoshop, CSP, and procreate in mind, but alternatives are available for other programs [on this page here](https://www.devinellekurtz.com/brushes)! great for backgrounds!

### people listing their favorite brushes:
- [CSP Materials](https://cspmaterials.carrd.co/#home): a carrd maintained by mephisto, sorts CSP resources by category!
- brush recomendations from [baoxie](https://baoxie.tumblr.com/) on tumblr (i love their three houses art and find inspiration in their style): [2024 post](https://baoxie.tumblr.com/post/743423356771172352/hello-what-brushes-do-you-use), [2022 post](https://baoxie.tumblr.com/post/694577162152443904/your-art-always-looks-so-calming-and-soft-i-cant)
- [my most used brushes (for clip studio paint)](https://rentry.co/240vo) brush recommendations from [240vo.bsky.social](https://bsky.app/profile/240vo.bsky.social), also contains process recommendations for lasso tool!

### brush packs:
- [Flat+Angular Brush Pack: 42 Custom Brushes for Photoshop & Procreate + Demo Videos](https://mikemccain.gumroad.com/l/ciZOH?layout=profile): PAID, costs $16-20. found out about this brush pack through [kat tsai (chuwenjie)](https://www.chuwenjie.com/), specifically when someone inquired about foliage/leaves. it's very geometric!
- [STIPPLE BEAST EVOLVED \| Brush Pack by TOMB of NULL](https://tombofnull.itch.io/stipple-beast-evolved-brush-pack): my friend [cat](https://catnatch.carrd.co/) sent this in a server... i'm obsessed with the texture on this. i can't remember if i downloaded it or not. but this is so up my alley. supports CSP, krita, and procreate.
