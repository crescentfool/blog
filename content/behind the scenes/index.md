hi there! this is a series where i talk about the process behind (some of) the things i've worked on. i write these because:

1. i value introspection and the insights that emerge from it
2. i love compiling resources and information about my process!

in a perfect world, i would be able to remember things perfectly, as they were. HOWEVER! i often find that specific details, or an accurate representation of how i felt at the time are lost to time... and what better way to counter that then to document things here in the present?

while these posts are primarily for myself (you would be surprised at how often i forget how to do certain things), i am sharing them with the hope that someone can learn from my experiences.

thanks for taking a look!
